About Us

The Red Sea Project is located in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and is being developed by Red Sea Global. It sits over 28,000km² of beautiful lands and waters and has more than 90 islands. It is going to be a luxury resort, which will include hotels, residential properties, entertainment ventures etc.

Main Benefits

Red Sea international airport

The Red Sea airport is expected to be completed in 2022 and service one million passengers in 2030
Landscape nursery

The 100 hectare Landscape Nursery will be one of the largest in the Middle East. They are selecting native species of plants, so the nursery can cut down on higher maintenance costs and water dependence.
Waste management facility

An international waste and recycling company, Averda was chosen to meet the Red Sea development companies goal of "zero waste to landfill"
Hotel destinations

The Red Sea Project will include hotels, residential properties, leisure facilities and entertainment ventures.

Construction village with a Central Medical facility

The Red Sea Project has marked the opening of its 700m2 Central Medical Facility within its Construction Village. The facility will have primary healthcare and emergency care.
Coastal village

The Coastal Village, is a residential and commercial area that will house the workers, staff and management of The Red Sea Project.
